Our Fertilizer Products

Scientifically Formulated Crop-Specific Fertilisers

Introduction

Crop productivity depends on one fundamental factor: balanced and crop-specific nutrition. At 3D Agro Fertilizer East Africa Limited, we manufacture fertilizers designed to supply essential nutrients in the right proportion for specific crops and soil conditions.

Our production approach is based on generative nutrient formulation. Rather than relying on generic blends, our fertilizers are developed through a process that combines crop nutrition science, soil analysis, and laboratory-guided nutrient balancing. This allows us to create fertilizer formulations that match the physiological needs of particular crops while supporting soil health and sustainable productivity.

Through our flagship product family MAVUNO, we provide specialized fertilizers developed for three of East Africa’s most important commercial crops:

  • Tobacco
  • Cotton
  • Tea

Each formulation is designed to support plant growth at critical stages of development, ensuring efficient nutrient uptake, improved plant vigor, and increased productivity.

The MAVUNO product line reflects our commitment to providing fertilizers that help farmers achieve higher yields, better crop quality, and more reliable agricultural performance.

MAVUNO CHAI FERTILIZER

A balanced fertilizer formulated for tea plantations, supporting sustained leaf production and plant health.

Tea cultivation requires continuous nutrient supply to maintain healthy bushes and support the ongoing production of fresh leaves. MAVUNO CHAI FERTILIZER delivers balanced nutrition that promotes sustained vegetative growth and consistent leaf production.

Particularly suited for tea plantations seeking to improve productivity while maintaining the quality of harvested tea leaves.
