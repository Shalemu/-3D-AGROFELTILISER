LEAF TOBACCO FERTILIZER

Optimized Nutrient Solutions

Balanced nutrition for vigorous leaf growth, structural strength, and high-quality tobacco leaves. Ensures tobacco plants grow evenly, produce large leaves, and achieve maximum crop productivity.

Key Benefits

  • Promotes vigorous leaf development
  • Supports balanced plant growth
  • Improves leaf quality for processing
  • Enhances crop productivity

Nutrient Composition

NPK Formula: 10-5-20 + TE

This balanced composition ensures tobacco plants receive essential nutrients for vegetative growth and leaf formation.

Recommended Use

Apply during early crop establishment and vegetative growth stages when plants require increased nutrient availability.

Adjust application rates based on soil conditions, crop management practices, and agronomic recommendations.
