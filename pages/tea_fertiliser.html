MAVUNO CHAI FERTILIZER

MAVUNO CHAI FERTILIZER

Tea plants require continuous nutrient supply to sustain vegetative growth and maintain productive tea bushes. The quality and quantity of harvested tea leaves depend heavily on the availability of balanced plant nutrition.

Formulation Strategy

  • Supplies essential nutrients for vigorous bush growth
  • Supports continuous leaf production
  • Improves efficiency of nutrient uptake
  • Maintains plant health with balanced macro and trace elements

Performance Outcomes

  • Sustained vegetative growth
  • Improved leaf quality for better tea production
  • Maintains high productivity over time
  • Ensures soil nutrient balance for long-term plantation health

Key Benefits

MAVUNO GREEN TEA Fertilizer is designed to promote healthy tea bush growth, improve leaf quality, and maintain plantation productivity.

Benefits

  • Promotes Healthy Bush Growth: Encourages vigorous vegetative growth that supports productive tea bushes.
  • Supports Continuous Leaf Production: Provides nutrients necessary for sustained tea harvesting.
  • Improves Leaf Quality: Supports the development of high-quality tea leaves suitable for processing.
  • Enhances Plantation Productivity: Helps maintain consistent yields across tea fields.

Nutrient Composition

NPK Formula: 17-7-10 + TE

Recommended Use

MAVUNO GREEN can be applied during active growth periods to support continuous leaf production and maintain healthy tea bushes. Application should be adjusted according to soil fertility conditions and agronomic advice.
