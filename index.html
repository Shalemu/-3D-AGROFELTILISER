About 3D Agro Fertilizer

3D Agro Fertilizer East Africa Limited is a fertilizer manufacturing company committed to improving agricultural productivity through scientifically developed nutrient solutions.

Agricultural soils vary widely in nutrient composition, structure, and fertility levels. Applying uniform fertilizer solutions across different soil conditions often leads to inefficient nutrient use, reduced crop performance, and long-term soil degradation.

Our approach addresses this challenge directly
  • Soil testing
  • Crop nutrient requirements
  • Laboratory formulation

We manufacture fertilizers based on a structured process that integrates soil testing, crop nutrient requirements, and laboratory formulation. This ensures that our products are aligned with actual farming conditions rather than assumptions.

Our objective is to support farmers with fertilizers that deliver measurable results, improve productivity, and contribute to sustainable agricultural systems.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

Our Process: Soil-Driven Fertilizer Formulation

Our fertilizers are developed through a structured process based on soil science and agronomic principles.

We begin with soil sampling and laboratory analysis to determine:

  • Macro nutrient levels (N, P, K)
  • Secondary nutrients (Ca, Mg, S)
  • Micronutrients (Zn, B, Fe, Mn)
  • Soil pH and nutrient availability
  • Organic matter content
  • Cation Exchange Capacity (CEC)

This data is used to develop a soil profile that identifies nutrient deficiencies, imbalances, and limiting factors affecting plant growth.

While NPK forms the base structure of our fertilizers, we adjust nutrient ratios and incorporate specific additives based on soil conditions and crop requirements.

This ensures:

  • Accurate nutrient supply
  • Improved nutrient use efficiency
  • Enhanced crop performance
  • Sustainable soil health
